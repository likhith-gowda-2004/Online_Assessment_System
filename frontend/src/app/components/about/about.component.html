About Us

OAS (Online Assessment System) is designed to simplify the process of conducting online assessments, quizzes, and coding challenges. Whether you're an educator, recruiter, or organization, our platform provides the tools you need to create, manage, and evaluate tests effortlessly.

Why Choose Us?

  • Easy-to-Use Interface: Create and manage tests with just a few clicks.
  • Comprehensive Question Types: Support for MCQs, coding challenges, and more.
  • Real-Time Evaluation: Instant feedback and auto-grading for coding and MCQ questions.
  • Secure Platform: Ensure the integrity of your assessments with advanced security features.

Our Mission

To empower educators, recruiters, and organizations with a reliable and efficient platform for conducting assessments that inspire learning, growth, and innovation.
